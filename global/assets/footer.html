About

Developed by experienced SLPs, talkon combines a management tool and parent application that helps SLPs encourage parents to take an active role in their child’s advancement, through intuitive at-home practicing. By turning practice into an enjoyable experience for the family, talkon supports sequential treatment with faster tangible results.
